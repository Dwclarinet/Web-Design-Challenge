This was our initial analysis. We thought we would find a relationship between GDP and Homeownership. Our initial theory was that a higher GDP would mean a higher homeownership percentage in the state.

We found that there is no direct relationship between GDP and homeownership and that there may be other factors involved. From here we decided that we wanted to see what relationships we could find using different variables and how GDP and homeownership effect these variables.

Something I found interesting with this project is that you can easily see how the recession effected different variables such as House Prices and Unemployment. You can also see that California and Wyoming took a hit to their GDP. Lastly, you can see that homeownership began decreasing in the states with high GDP when the recession hit.
