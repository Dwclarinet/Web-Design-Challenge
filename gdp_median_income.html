In this analysis, we thought we thought we would find a relation between a state's GDP and its median income. The higher a state's GDP, the higher the state's median income.

There was no relationship in this analysis; GDP has no relationship with median income. We can prove this because Vermont has the highest median income but the lowest GDP however California has the second highest median income but has the highest GDP.
